    E&P Group: Excellence in Marine Logistics

    E&P Group has established itself as a strategic leader in the maritime transport and comprehensive logistics management sector. With a strong operational presence in more than 20 strategic ports, the company facilitates connections between international markets through an efficient, high-capacity transport network.

    Specialization in Freight

    The versatility of its fleet and technical team enables the company to handle a wide variety of goods, adapting to the specific requirements of each industry:

    • General cargo: palletized goods, manufactured products, and consumer goods.
    • Bulk cargo: transportation of solid and liquid raw materials.
    • Special and oversized cargo: logistics for heavy machinery and industrial infrastructure.
    • Project cargo: management of complex shipments requiring detailed technical planning.

    Our Core Pillars

    E&P Group’s operational philosophy is based on three pillars that ensure service excellence:

    • Trust: We build long-term relationships based on transparency and full cargo traceability at every stage of the journey.
    • Security: We apply the highest international port and maritime security standards to mitigate risks and safeguard our clients’ assets.
    • Customer Commitment: We understand that every shipment is critical. Therefore, we provide tailored solutions, optimized transit times, and proactive support to ensure cargo arrives at its destination on time and efficiently.
